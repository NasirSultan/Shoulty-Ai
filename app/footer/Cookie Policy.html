1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide you with a better experience, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our Service.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function (login, security, payment processing)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website

4. Your Consent & Control

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner asking for your consent. You can:

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our Service.
