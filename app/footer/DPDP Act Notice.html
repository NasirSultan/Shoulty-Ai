This notice applies to data principals (individuals) located in India and supplements our main Privacy Policy. It describes your rights under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

3. Your Rights as a Data Principal (Section 12-15)

  • Right to Access: Obtain confirmation of whether we are processing your personal data and request a summary.

  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Have your complaints addressed by our designated Data Protection Officer.
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate another individual to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity.
